Testimonials IIT

The Sfax International Institute of Technology (IIT), driven by a commitment to quality and excellence, has successfully established a name and reputation in both the national and international academic world. Over the years, IIT has earned a reputation for rigor and, in particular, for its international outlook. Its primary mission remains to equip its graduates with the best possible chances of meeting the new demands of an international professional world, thanks to the quality of its teaching and numerous certifications
