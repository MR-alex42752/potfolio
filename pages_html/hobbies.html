Hobbies

Football

Football is a team sport that brings you into contact with many personalities. Just like school, playing football contributes to learning about life in society. As a member of a team, you develop a sense of community and the desire to surpass yourself for your teammates (well, in most cases!). Some seem not to grasp this notion, but that's another debate....

playing guitar

Playing the guitar enhances memory and improves concentration. Practicing scales, chords, memorizing, and reproducing melodies contributes to a musician's brain development. As a result, a child who learns to play the guitar at a young age will be more attentive in school and will be better able to remember what they learn. Similarly, playing the guitar is a fantastic way to stay young at heart!

Coding

Learning to code is investing in your personal development, ensuring your professional fulfillment, while also having the ability to make a positive impact. Yes, it's that simple! If you're still not convinced, this article is for you. Among these skills is a whole range of abilities that stimulate the intellect: problem-solving, critical thinking, perseverance, rigor, lateral thinking... In other words, if you enjoy challenges, learning to code will not disappoint you!
